AI Tutor App

Personal AI learning assistant for students worldwide.

👀 1.2K Views 💬 34 Comments 📅 Posted Jan 2026 🌎 Category: AI & Tech
🎯 Problem Statement

Students struggle with personalized learning. Traditional education fails to adapt to individual learning speeds and styles, leaving many students behind.

💡 Proposed Solution

An AI-powered tutoring app that adapts to each student's pace, identifies weak areas, and provides personalized practice problems and explanations in real-time.

Target Audience

Students aged 10–25, parents, online learning platforms.

Revenue Model

Freemium subscription — $9/mo for premium features.

Tech Stack

React Native, Python, OpenAI API, Firebase.

Stage

MVP Ready — Seeking Co-founder & Seed Funding.
