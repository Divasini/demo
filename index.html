FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before diving in.

How many ideas can I share for free? ＋

On the Free plan, you can submit up to 3 startup ideas. They'll be visible to the entire StartupSphere community and receive votes, comments, and feedback just like any other idea.

What happens after 3 idea submissions? ＋

Once you've submitted 3 ideas on the Free plan, you'll be prompted to upgrade to Pro for unlimited submissions. Your existing ideas remain live and continue receiving community engagement.

Can I upgrade anytime? ＋

Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade from Free to Pro Monthly or Premium Yearly at any point from your dashboard. Your plan activates instantly and all premium features become available right away.

Can investors see my ideas? ＋

Pro and Premium members get investor visibility, meaning your ideas are listed in our curated investor feed. Our verified network of angel investors and VCs actively browses this feed to discover early-stage startups.

Is StartupSphere suitable for students? ＋

Definitely. Many of our most-loved ideas come from students. StartupSphere is built for anyone with a bold idea — whether you're in college, at your first job, or an experienced founder. The Free plan is a great way to start with zero risk.
